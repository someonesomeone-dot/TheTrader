The Trader

How To Play

In this game, you and 3 bots are each assigned a role:

  • Businessman: Aim for the highest total sum.
  • Diplomat: Your goal is a middle total (neither highest nor lowest).
  • Athlete: Aim for the lowest total sum.

The game lasts 10 rounds. In each round, you draw a card (from 1–100) which is added to your hand (a persistent pool). Then, select any card from your hand and choose a bot to attempt a trade. When you initiate a trade, the bot (without knowing your offered card) will decide whether it’s willing to swap based solely on its own card. If the bot is willing, the trade is made; if not, your selected card is automatically kept (finalized) and added to your total. Additionally, you may manually “Keep Selected Card” up to 2 times per game.
